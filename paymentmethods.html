Digital Wallets

Digital wallets have become an extremely popular way to pay online and in person. When customers use a digital wallet, their payment information is stored securely so that they can quickly and safely choose their payment method to check out at a POS or on the web. Most digital wallets utilize advanced security measures that make them the safest way for the customer to pay and the ISV or merchant to hold little to no liability for sensitive customer data.


Digital wallets will automatically be enabled with the selection of card payment methods in your iframe: paymentOptions: ['credit_card'].
